About

Research overview

I work on post-training large language models, especially for code and reasoning tasks. My research centers on reinforcement fine-tuning, evaluation, and training methods for better generalization and reliability.

Before Fsoft AI Center, I worked at VinBigdata Institute on multimodal language models and audio-text alignment, and at BKAI on optimization algorithms, topic models, and combinatorial optimization.
