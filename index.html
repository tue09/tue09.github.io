About

Research overview

I work on post-training large language models, with emphasis on code and reasoning tasks. My research spans reinforcement fine-tuning, evaluation, and training strategies that improve generalization, reliability, and alignment.

Before joining Fsoft AI Center, I worked at VinBigdata Institute on multimodal language models and audio-text alignment. I also conducted research at BKAI on optimization algorithms, topic models, and combinatorial optimization.

Current interests

  • Post-training pipelines for large language models
  • Reinforcement fine-tuning for code and reasoning
  • Evaluation and reliability on long-horizon tasks
  • Alignment-aware model improvement methods
