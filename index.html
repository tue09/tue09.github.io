About

A simple overview

I am an AI researcher based in Hanoi, with work spanning post-training for large language models, reinforcement fine-tuning, and AI for code. My recent projects focus on making models more reliable on reasoning-heavy and code-heavy tasks.

Before my current role at Fsoft AI Center, I worked at VinBigdata Institute on multimodal language models and speech-language systems, and I was involved in research at BKAI on optimization methods for neural networks and combinatorial optimization.
