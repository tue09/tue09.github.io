About

Research Profile

I am an AI researcher based in Hanoi, Vietnam. My work centers on post-training large language models, particularly reinforcement fine-tuning methods that improve alignment, robustness, and out-of-distribution generalization for code and reasoning.

I have worked across both academic and applied settings, including AI research roles at Fsoft AI Center, VinBigdata Institute, and BKAI labs. I am actively building a long-term research agenda at the intersection of reliable LLM systems, optimization, and practical deployment.
